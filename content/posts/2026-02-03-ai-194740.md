ORamaVR has recently raised $4.5 million in seed funding to enhance its XR platform aimed at revolutionizing medical device training. This innovative platform is designed to address significant challenges in surgical planning, navigation, rehabilitation, and therapy, making healthcare training more efficient and accessible.

With the increasing recognition of XR's potential in the medical field, ORamaVR is positioning itself to lead the charge in this burgeoning industry. The funding will enable the company to expand its capabilities and further develop its technology, which could transform how medical professionals are trained.

As the use of XR in medicine continues to grow, it raises an important question: how will this technology shape the future of healthcare training and patient outcomes?
