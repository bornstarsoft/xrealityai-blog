In a groundbreaking presentation at the CAPA Airline Leader Summit, Meta showcased how extended reality (XR) and artificial intelligence (AI) are revolutionizing the aviation industry. These technologies are not just futuristic concepts; they are practical tools that airlines are deploying today to enhance training, operations, and passenger experiences.

Airlines are leveraging XR for crew training, creating realistic simulations that improve safety and reduce costs. Meanwhile, AI is optimizing operations by predicting needs and personalizing passenger engagement. Innovations like immersive in-flight entertainment and bring-your-own-device strategies are making travel more enjoyable without complicating aircraft design.

Looking ahead, Meta's development of next-generation augmented reality devices, such as Orion smart glasses, suggests a future where real-time information enhances both crew workflows and passenger interactions.
