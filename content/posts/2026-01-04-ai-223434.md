The Jeonnam Pavilion at CES 2026 is set to make waves by showcasing twelve innovative technologies from local companies, emphasizing Jeollanam-do's commitment to global market innovation. This year's CES theme, "Innovator Show Up," resonates deeply with the province's vision, particularly in areas like Artificial Intelligence, Energy Transition, and Smart Mobility.

Notably, four of the participating companiesIIST, Korea Optron, Newjak, and Turbine Crewhave earned the prestigious CES 2026 Innovation Award, highlighting their technological excellence. Jeollanam-do is not just about showcasing; it aims to facilitate the global expansion of its local companies through significant investment agreements with international partners during the event.

As we look forward to CES 2026, it's exciting to ponder how these innovations will shape the future of technology and global collaboration. Will Jeollanam-do's participation inspire other regions to follow suit in showcasing their technological advancements?
