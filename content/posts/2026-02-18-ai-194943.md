The WAVES Creators' Corner, inaugurated by Union Minister Shri Ashwini Vaishnaw at the India AI Impact Summit 2026, represents a significant leap forward in showcasing the transformative potential of Artificial Intelligence within the Animation, VFX, Gaming, Comics, and Extended Reality (AVGC-XR) sectors. This innovative workspace not only highlights cutting-edge AI technologies but also serves as a launchpad for 51 startups, empowering them to present their AI-driven solutions to a global audience.

During the event, the Minister emphasized the importance of integrating AI into creative fields, illustrating how technology can bridge gaps for talent from rural India, enabling them to establish world-class startups. The Pavilion featured groundbreaking innovations, including real-time game development tools and AI-powered newsrooms, which are set to redefine the landscape of media and entertainment.

As we look to the future, the WAVES Creators' Corner stands as a beacon of opportunity for aspiring creators and entrepreneurs. Will this initiative inspire a new wave of innovation in the AVGC-XR ecosystem?
