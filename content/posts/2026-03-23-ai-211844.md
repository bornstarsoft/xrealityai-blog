KONAMI is stepping into the world of Picross with its upcoming release, "Picross S: Konami Antiques Edition," set to launch on April 30 for the Nintendo Switch. This game promises to be a nostalgic trip for fans, featuring 585 puzzles inspired by over 80 classic Konami titles from the 8-bit and 16-bit eras, including beloved franchises like Castlevania, Metal Gear, and Contra.

Players will engage in the familiar Picross gameplay, where they solve numerical puzzles to reveal pixel art images from Konami's rich gaming history. With a variety of puzzle types, including Mega Picross and Color Picross, this edition offers something for both newcomers and seasoned Picross veterans. The inclusion of mouse and touchscreen controls ensures a versatile gaming experience, catering to different player preferences.

As the ninth installment in the Picross S series, this game not only expands the franchise but also highlights the enduring appeal of classic gaming. Will this new addition rekindle your love for Konami's iconic titles? Stay tuned as we await its release!
