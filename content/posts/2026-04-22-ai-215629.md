The Indian Institute of Creative Technologies (IICT) is making significant strides in establishing a robust AVGC-XR ecosystem, as evidenced by its recent partnerships announced at the India Gaming Show 2026. By signing multiple Memoranda of Understanding (MoUs) with industry leaders, IICT is paving the way for a future-ready digital content landscape in India, focusing on education, skilling, and research across emerging domains like esports and immersive technologies.

One of the standout collaborations is with The Zenfluencers, aimed at developing structured esports education that includes globally recognized certification programs and industry-led workshops. This initiative not only enhances employability but also provides students with invaluable global exposure and engagement within the esports value chain. Additionally, partnerships with YOLOgram Style Pvt. Ltd. and Eruditio Consultants further emphasize the commitment to curriculum development and nationwide outreach, ensuring that opportunities in the AVGC-XR sector are accessible to all.

As IICT CEO Dr. Vishwas Deoskar highlighted, strong industry-academia collaboration is crucial for shaping the future of creative technology in India. With these initiatives, India is poised to become a global hub for digital content and immersive innovation. How will these developments influence the next generation of digital creators in India?
